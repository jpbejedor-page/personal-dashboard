# Terms and Conditions

**Last Updated:** June 4, 2026

## Lending Business Terms

### General Terms
1. All loans must be documented in the system
2. Borrowers must provide valid identification
3. Payment schedules must be agreed upon before loan disbursement

### Interest Rates
- Standard loans: As agreed between parties
- Simple loans: Flexible terms, charges added as needed

### Payment Terms
- Payments can be made: Daily, Weekly, Semi-Monthly, or Monthly
- Late payments may incur additional charges
- Partial payments are accepted for simple loans

### Default Policy
1. Grace period: 7 days after due date
2. Late fee: As specified in loan agreement
3. Legal action may be taken for prolonged defaults

## Privacy Policy
- All borrower information is kept confidential
- Data is stored securely in Firebase
- Access is restricted to authorized users only

## Modifications
These terms may be updated at any time. Check this document regularly for changes.
